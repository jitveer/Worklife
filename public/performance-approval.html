Section 3: Core Competencies

S.No. Professional Competencies Rating
1 Communication
Communication demonstrates an understanding of the views of others. Communicates in a clear and logical manner using appropriate language. Listens attentively to views and issues of others. Adapts appropriate methods of communication for each situation. Conveys and receives information effectively and builds positive working relationships. Body language is positive and open. Written communication is professional and business appropriate.
2 Decision Making
Is able to analyze situations, diagnose problems, identify the key issues, establish and evaluate alternative courses of action and produce a logical, practical and acceptable solution. Is able to make effective decisions on a day-to-day basis, taking ownership of decisions, demonstrating sound judgement in escalating issues where necessary.
3 Quality Orientation And Accuracy
Diligently attends to details and pursues quality in accomplishing tasks. Performs tasks with care and is thorough. Makes few errors. Checks work to ensure accuracy and completeness. Reviews work before submitting. Is alert and takes care of details that are easy to overlook or dismiss as unimportant.
4 Initiative
Identifies what needs to be done and does it before being asked or before the situation requires it. Does more than what is normally required in a situation. Seeks out others involved in a situation to learn their perspectives. Takes independent action to change the direction of events. Comes up with new ideas to alter current operational processes that may be old or ineffective. Goes the extra mile and is keen to be part of projects within the Company that may be beyond expectations from him/her.
5 Technical Skills
Demonstrates depth of knowledge and skill in a technical area. Effectively applies technical knowledge to solve a range of problems. Possesses strong knowledge and skill in their technical area. Develops technical solutions to new or highly complex problems that cannot be solved using existing methods or approaches. Stays informed about changes in their area of expertise/knowledge.
Total

Section 4: Behavioral Competencies

S.No Competency Rating
1 Team Work Works cooperatively and flexibly with other members of the team with a full understanding of the role to be played as a team member to achieve a common goal. Acts in a supportive manner to the team. Encourages team unity through sharing information or expertise, working together to solve problems, and putting team success first.
2 Planning and Organizing Plans and organizes their own work through a clear understanding of their objectives and targets. Planning, organizing and/or monitoring one’s own work and ensures achievement of desired results. Requires little follow up and keeps manager informed of progress. Identifies what resources and people are required to get the work done and plans these well in advance – not last minute.
3 Adaptability / Flexibility Is open to different and new ways of doing things and is willing to modify their preferred way of doing things. Demonstrates openness to new organizational structures, procedures, and technology. Switches to a different strategy when an initially selected one is unsuccessful. Is open to other people’s point of view – adapts easily to organizational changes.
4 Self Confidence Has faith in one’s own ideas and capability to be successful and is willing to stand by their point of view if it is logical. Is confident of own ability to accomplish goals. Presents themselves crisply and impressively. Is willing to speak up to the right person or group at the right time, when he/she disagrees with a decision or strategy. Approaches challenging tasks with a “can-do” attitude.
5 Creativity and Innovation Develops fresh ideas that provide solutions to all types of workplace challenges. Sees old problems in new ways and has new approaches to solving those problems. Contributes original ideas in brainstorming sessions. Finds ways to turn the idea into reality. Experiments with new ideas, methodologies, and procedures. Is willing to try new ways of doing things.
Total
